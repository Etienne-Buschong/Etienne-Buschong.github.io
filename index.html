Projects

Collaborative Course Study

This project was written by four fellow students and me in the scope of an university course. Each group had to come up with an idea of an useful information system. We decided to implement a university-internal information system for fellow students to collaborate. While the idea was not highly original, we all had a clear idea what features were needed. This allowed us to implement the system with many features in a relatively short amount of time.

The result is a platform with account-based authentication, real-time chat capabilities, file-sharing and much more. The frontend is implemented in Angular as an SPA. The backend is implemented in PHP. Both the client and server relied only on a small amount of third- party dependencies to increase our learning-effect.

 

Training Manager App

This project was written by four fellow students and me in the scope of a two week practical training for the university course Mobile Computing. To make the practical training more realistic and useful our professor found a voluntary member of a soccer club who asked for a android training app, to create, schedule and share soccer training plans with the colleagues in the club.

The result was an android app which allowed exactly that. Coaches can create training plans with details like the target group, required materials, plan length and much more. Additionally an installation of the materials can be created via a whiteboard. Other coaches can search for training plans by filtering their query according to their needs. The corresponding backend was written in Node.js with Express and Mongo.db.
